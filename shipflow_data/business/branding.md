# Brand Context

## Voice

Use direct, practical, learning-oriented language. Explain concrete outcomes instead of generic productivity claims.

## Claim Boundaries

Public copy can claim structured video learning, timestamped notes, playlists, and review workflows when backed by app behavior. Claims about AI, automation, savings, security, availability, or pricing require explicit evidence in the product contracts or claim register.

## Language

The project has English and French public surfaces. Keep translations aligned in meaning and maintain natural French accents in user-facing French copy.
